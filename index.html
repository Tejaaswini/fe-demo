Santander® Private Client

Enjoy exclusive benefits and a relationship banker who takes care of your

Eligibility for a
Santander Private Client Checking Account

Client must maintain a minimum combined balance of at least $250,000.1

This combined balance includes consumer and certain business deposit products, and eligible investments held with Santander Investment Services.2

Santander Private Client Checking benefits

Priority services

  • Dedicated customer service line
  • Day-to-day banking needs fully managed by a relationship banker
  • An on-demand team of financial specialists to provide support and guidance

Exclusive discounts

  • Rate discounts on eligible consumer loans with automatic payment from any Santander Private Client Checking Account
  • No charge for Santander Private Client style or Santander Wallet Exclusive checks, or a 50% discount on alternate designs
  • No charge for the smallest size safe deposit box and a 50% discount on other sizes

Extras

  • No Monthly Fee.
  • No fees for incoming domestic or international wires or domestic outgoing wires
  • No fee for money orders or official bank checks
  • No fees for Overdraft Protection Transfers
  • Unlimited ATM Surcharge Fee rebates for ATM fees charged by other institutions when using non-Santander ATMs
  • For additional details, please refer to the Santander Private Client Fee Schedule.
